Round 1 Submission

The submission for Round 1 is a slide deck spanning at most 5 slides (excluding the title slide) that outlines your technical idea. A good presentation will contain the following:

  • Information about the team, their project roles, and any mentors/advisors to the project
  • Explanation of the problematic issue, and any relevant statistics about market size (if a commercial idea), individuals affected (if a civic/non-profit innovation), or any metrics you think are appropriate
  • Technical Explanation of idea and footnotes/references if applicable
  • Timeline for next 6 months outlining the next steps to produce a Minimal Viable Product, and relevant costs
